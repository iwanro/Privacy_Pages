Privacy Policy

Beekeeping Tracker ("we", "the app", "the service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Important: By using this app, you agree to the collection and use of data described in this policy.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Data You Provide

  • Account Data: Name, email address (if applicable)
  • Subscription Data: Premium purchase information, subscription status
  • Beekeeping Data: Apiary, hive, queen, activity, harvest, and treatment information
  • Usage Data: Language preferences, notification settings, app theme

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Device Data: Phone/tablet model, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features used, time spent in app, system errors
  • Location Data: General device location (only if permitted and for apiary coordinates)

1.3 Payment Data

Payment Processing: Payments are processed through RevenueCat and app stores (Google Play Store, Apple App Store). We do not store or process your credit card details.
  • Payment information is collected directly by the app store
  • RevenueCat receives only purchase confirmation and subscription details
  • See RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • See app store privacy policies:
    • Google Play Privacy Policy
    • Apple App Store Privacy Policy

2. How We Use Your Data

2.1 Primary Service Purpose

  • To provide core app features (apiary, hive, activity management)
  • To provide premium features (voice dictation, data export)
  • To manage premium subscription and billing
  • To send important notifications about hives (inspection reminders, treatments)

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Usage analysis to improve features
  • Error debugging and app stability maintenance
  • Development of new features based on user feedback

2.3 Communications

  • To send feature updates
  • To inform about service changes
  • For technical support (if you contact us)

3. Data Sharing

3.1 We Don't Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or transfer your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3.2 Service Providers

We share data only with necessary service providers:

  • RevenueCat - for premium subscription management
  • Google/Apple - for payment processing through app stores
  • Notification Services - Firebase Cloud Messaging for push notifications
  • Analytics Services - for app stability monitoring (no personal data)

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose data if required to:

  • Comply with law (court order, subpoena)
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Ensure user safety

4. Data Security

  • Local Storage: All beekeeping data is stored locally on your device in the Isar database (encrypted)
  • No Central Server: We do not store your data on our central servers
  • User Backup: You are responsible for backing up your data through the export function
  • Encryption: Data is protected by encryption in transit and at rest

5. Children's Data

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not intentionally collect data from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

6. Your Rights

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: You can view all data stored in the app
  • Export: You can export your data in CSV or JSON format (premium feature)
  • Delete: You can delete any data directly from the app
  • Unsubscribe: You can cancel your premium subscription at any time

6.2 Data Portability

You can export all your data in standard format (CSV/JSON) and use it in other applications.

7. Premium Subscription and Payments

7.1 General Information

  • Premium subscription offers unlimited features
  • Payment is processed through the app store (Google Play/App Store)
  • Prices are displayed before purchase
  • Subscriptions renew automatically until cancelled

7.2 Renewal Policy

  • Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of the current period
  • You can cancel anytime through the app store
  • Cancellation does not affect the current paid period
  • No refunds for partial periods

7.3 Subscription Management

  • Go to "Settings" → "Subscription" → "Manage Subscription"
  • Or directly through the app store

8. Privacy and Notifications

8.1 GPS Coordinates

  • We collect GPS coordinates only to mark apiary locations
  • Location data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not track real-time location
  • You can add coordinates manually

8.2 Microphone (Voice Dictation)

  • We use microphone only for voice dictation
  • Recording is processed locally on your device
  • We do not store audio recordings
  • Feature is available only for premium subscribers

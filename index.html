AppointMate Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026 | Effective Date: May 1, 2026

AppointMate ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data

AppointMate collects the following personal information:

  • Contact Information: Names, phone numbers from your device contacts (with your explicit permission)
  • Appointment Data: Appointment details including dates, times, descriptions, and client information
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent in app, crash logs

Android Permissions

We request the following Android permissions:

  • READ_CONTACTS — To import client information from your contacts
  • SEND_SMS — To send appointment confirmation and reminder SMS messages
  • CALL_PHONE — To initiate calls to clients directly from the app
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — To send you appointment reminders
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — To reschedule notifications after device restart
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — To ensure precise notification timing

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Manage and track your appointments
  • Send SMS reminders to your clients
  • Provide notification alerts for upcoming appointments
  • Improve app functionality and fix bugs
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

All appointment data is stored locally on your device using the Isar database. We do not store your appointment data on our servers unless you explicitly use backup features.

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Local database encryption on supported devices
  • Secure data transmission for any network operations
  • Regular security updates

Important: You are responsible for maintaining device security (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We are not liable for data loss due to unauthorized device access.

4. Third-Party Services

Adapty (Subscription Management)

We use Adapty for subscription management. Adapty may collect:

  • Purchase receipt data
  • Subscription status information
  • Anonymous usage analytics

Adapty Privacy Policy: https://adapty.io/privacy-policy/

Firebase Cloud Messaging

We use Firebase for push notifications. Firebase may collect device tokens and notification delivery data.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only:

  • With service providers who perform services on our behalf (e.g., Adapty, Firebase)
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With your explicit consent

6. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • Appointment Data: Until you delete it from the app
  • Usage Analytics: Up to 12 months
  • Support Communications: Up to 2 years

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Restrict: Limit how we use your data

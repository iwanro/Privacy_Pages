# Privacy Policy for Baby Tracker

**Last updated: March 20, 2026**

## Introduction

Baby Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Baby Tracker mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally
The App stores the following data locally on your device:
- Baby profiles (name, birth date)
- Sleep sessions (start time, end time)
- Feeding sessions (start time, end time, type, amount)
- Diaper changes (time, type)
- Solid food records (date, ingredients, notes)
- Pumping sessions (date, amount, duration)
- Medicine records (name, dosage, date)
- Growth records (height, weight, head circumference)
- Temperature records (value, date, location)
- Activity records (type, duration, notes)

### Data Storage
- **All data is stored locally on your device** using Isar database
- **No data is sent to external servers**
- **No account registration is required**
- **No cloud sync functionality**

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Analytics data
- Advertising identifiers

## Data Sharing
We do **NOT** share any data with third parties. All data remains on your device.

## Data Security
- All data is stored locally and encrypted by the device's operating system
- No internet connection is required for the app to function
- We do not transmit any data over the network

## Data Retention
- Data is retained until you manually delete it or uninstall the app
- You can delete individual records at any time through the app interface

## Children's Privacy
This App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. The App is designed to help parents track their baby's activities, but all data remains on the parent's device.

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access your data (view within the app)
- Delete your data (remove individual records or uninstall the app)
- Export your data (sharing feature available in the app)

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

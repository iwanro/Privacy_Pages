2. Data We Collect

The App collects and processes the following categories of personal and non-personal data:

2.1 Location Data (GPS Coordinates)

  • What: Precise GPS latitude/longitude coordinates, accuracy readings, and reverse-geocoded address information.
  • How: Collected via the device's GPS hardware when you use the "capture delivery location" or "set container location" features.
  • Frequency: Only at the moment you explicitly trigger location capture. The App does NOT track your location in the background or continuously.
  • Purpose: To record the geographic location where containers are delivered, placed, or picked up, for rental management purposes.

2.2 Contacts Data

  • What: Contact names and phone numbers.
  • How: Collected via the Android Contact Picker — only when you manually select a contact from your address book.
  • Purpose: To associate clients with rentals and to enable SMS notifications via the device's default SMS application.
  • Note: The App reads contact information you explicitly choose. It does NOT scan or upload your entire contact list.

2.3 Container and Rental Data

  • What: Container names, descriptions, statuses, rental start/end dates, prices, payment statuses, and associated client references.
  • Purpose: Core app functionality — inventory management, rental tracking, payment monitoring, and PDF contract generation.

2.4 Device Identifiers and Technical Information

  • What: Device model, operating system version, app version, FCM (Firebase Cloud Messaging) push notification token.
  • Purpose: Push notifications (rental reminders, status updates), crash diagnostics, and app functionality.

3. How Data Is Collected

Data is collected through the following mechanisms:

  1. Direct user input: Container names, descriptions, prices, rental details entered via the app UI.
  2. Device permission prompts: GPS location (when you tap "capture location") and contacts (when you use the contact picker).
  3. Automatic technical data: FCM push token for notification delivery via Firebase.

4. Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Article 6)

The App processes your data under the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract (Article 6(1)(b)): The data is necessary to provide the core functionalities of the App — managing container inventory, tracking rentals, generating PDF contracts, and sending SMS notifications.
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): Location and contacts data are processed only after you grant explicit permission via the Android permission system. You may withdraw consent at any time via device settings.
  • Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)): Anonymous crash reporting and analytics to improve app stability.

5. Data Storage and Security

Primary storage: All container, rental, client, and configuration data is stored locally on your device using an embedded Isar database. No cloud servers, sync servers, or remote databases are used for your core business data.

Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): The App uses Firebase (Google) for push notifications only. FCM token is transmitted to Firebase servers solely for notification delivery. No container, rental, or contact data is transmitted to Firebase.

Encryption: The local database is device-native and benefits from Android's built-in file-level encryption when the device is locked.

Data retention: Data remains on your device until you choose to delete it via the app interface or uninstall the App. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

6. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google LLC): Used solely for push notification delivery. FCM token shared. Data policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google Geocoding API (via geolocator/geocoding plugin): Converts GPS coordinates to human-readable addresses. Only the GPS coordinates are transmitted to Google at the moment of geocoding. Data policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • SMS (via default SMS app): When you send SMS notifications, the App opens your device's default SMS application with pre-filled content. The App itself does not send or intercept SMS messages.
  • No other third parties: The App does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or any other third-party data collection tools.

The Developer does NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

10. Third-Party Permissions (Android)

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: Used ONLY when you explicitly trigger location capture for container delivery/pickup. Never used in the background.

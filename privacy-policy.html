🔒 Privacy Policy

SmartSummarizer AI Chrome Extension

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

1. What Data Do We Collect?

  • ✗ NO Personal Data - We don't collect your name, email, IP address, or any personally identifiable information
  • ✗ NO Content Storage - We don't store the content you summarize
  • ✗ NO Browsing History - We don't track which websites you visit
  • ✗ NO Analytics - We don't use tracking, cookies, or analytics services

2. How Your Data Is Processed

What happens when you generate a summary:

  1. You click the extension icon and choose to summarize a page
  2. The content (page text or selected text) is extracted from your browser
  3. Content is sent via HTTPS to our API backend (smartsummarizer-api.vercel.app)
  4. Our backend forwards the content to AI services (Google, Anthropic, OpenAI, Groq)
  5. The AI returns the summary to our backend
  6. The summary is displayed in your browser
  7. The content is immediately discarded - not saved in databases or logs

3. What Is Stored Locally?

The only data stored in your browser (using chrome.storage.local):

  • Daily summary count - To enforce the 60 summaries/day limit for free users
  • Pro status - Whether you've upgraded to Pro (boolean true/false)
  • License key - Your ExtensionPay license key (only if you're a Pro user)

💡 All this data stays on your device. We never access it without your permission.

4. Extension Permissions Explained

Our extension requests minimal permissions to function:

📁 storage

Purpose: Save your usage counter and Pro status locally in your browser.

Data: Only stores: usageCount, lastReset, isPro, licenseKey

🔑 activeTab

Purpose: Access the current page content to summarize it.

When: Only when YOU click the extension icon. We don't access tabs in the background.

⚙️ scripting

Purpose: Inject the sidebar UI into web pages.

What: Only injects our isolated iframe sidebar. Doesn't modify page behavior.

🔔 notifications

Purpose: Show notifications for major extension updates.

Frequency: Rarely used - only for important updates.

🌐 host_permissions

Purpose: Communicate with our API backend.

Domains: Only *.vercel.app - our secure backend API. No other domains.

7. YouTube Videos

When summarizing YouTube videos:

  • We fetch the publicly available transcript from YouTube's servers
  • We don't access your watch history or personal YouTube data
  • The transcript is sent to AI services the same way as page content

8. Data Security

  • ✅ All communication uses HTTPS encryption
  • ✅ API keys are stored securely on our backend (not in the extension)
  • ✅ We don't log or store any content on our servers
  • ✅ Local storage data is protected by Chrome's storage security model
